# 📊 Best Buy Sales & Customer Analysis  

## 🎯 Project Objective  
In today’s fast-paced retail environment, understanding **sales trends, customer behavior, and profitability** is crucial for business success. This project leverages **Power BI & SQL** to uncover key insights into **Best Buy’s sales performance, customer retention patterns, and shipping preferences**, empowering data-driven decision-making.  

---  

## 📂 Dataset Overview  
This project utilizes **real-world sales data** from Best Buy, covering transactions across multiple regions. The dataset includes:  
✅ **Order ID** – Unique identifier for each transaction  
✅ **Customer ID** – Helps track purchase behavior and retention trends  
✅ **Product Category & Sub-category** – Identifies top-performing and underperforming products  
✅ **Sales & Profit Data** – Measures financial performance  
✅ **Order Date & Ship Mode** – Analyzes seasonal trends and delivery efficiency  
✅ **State & Region** – Maps revenue distribution across locations  

---  

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) & Business Questions  
🔹 **What are the highest revenue-generating product categories?**  
🔹 **Which customer segments contribute most to sales, and what is their retention rate?**  
🔹 **How do seasonal trends impact revenue, and when is peak sales season?**  
🔹 **Which shipping mode is most efficient and cost-effective?**  
🔹 **How does revenue distribution vary across states and regions?**  

---  

## 🔄 Process & Approach  
1️⃣ **Data Collection & Cleaning:** Extracted raw data, handled missing values, and standardized formats using **Power BI & SQL**.  
2️⃣ **Data Transformation:** Employed **DAX measures and SQL queries** to derive key metrics such as **profit margins, churn rate, and average order value**.  
3️⃣ **Data Visualization:** Designed **interactive dashboards** to track sales trends, customer behavior, and product demand.  
4️⃣ **Business Strategy Insights:** Provided actionable recommendations based on **customer retention, seasonal trends, and logistics performance**.  

---  

## 📊 Dashboard Overview  
📌 **Sales & Profit Analysis** – Identifies top-selling categories and revenue drivers  
📌 **Customer Insights** – Retention, churn, and buying behavior trends  
📌 **Product Performance** – Highlights best-sellers and underperforming items  
📌 **Shipping & Delivery Performance** – Evaluates the impact of shipping methods on sales  
📌 **Seasonal Trends & Forecasting** – Predicts peak sales periods for inventory planning  

---  

## 📊 Key Findings & Business Impact  
✔ **Revenue Concentration:** A small set of products drive the majority of sales, signaling opportunities to expand weaker segments.  
✔ **Customer Retention Insights:** Repeat customers account for a significant portion of revenue—implementing **personalized marketing campaigns** could boost loyalty.  
✔ **Seasonal Demand Peaks:** Q4 (October-December) experiences the highest sales volume—**strategic promotions** during this period can maximize profits.  
✔ **Shipping Efficiency:** **Expedited Shipping customers tend to place higher-value orders**, suggesting a potential upsell opportunity.  
✔ **High Churn Rate in November:** A noticeable customer drop-off suggests a need for **pricing adjustments or enhanced customer service efforts**.  
✔ **Regional Performance:** **California & New York** lead in sales, but **expansion opportunities exist in underperforming regions** through localized marketing.
